LEARNING PLAN


What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

My long term goal is to start a business that focuses on building digital products that address social issues I care about.

A description of your own strengths and limitations in relation to learning

I believe my strengths include conscientiousness, communication skills, and analytical skills. Things I would like to improve on are my time management, stress management, and project management skills.

What skills (non-technical - human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I would like to develop my time-management skills.

A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme.

There are a couple of things I will try to do to manage the workload in this programme. First, I will try to be reflective of my stress levels. For example, when I am very stressed out I tend to eat a lot of junk food. So if I start doing that it is a reminder that maybe I am quite stressed out. When I am stressed out, I will attempt to take a short break and meditate. Meditation is my method of destressing. Second, I will try to keep a record of the time I take to work on each section and to take sufficient breaks in between. Third, I will try to ask for help when I am stuck on something for too long.

A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way

I will seek help in a timely way by evaluating a few things. First, I will try to judge whether it is a situation where I don’t know what I don’t know, or if it is a case of where I know what I don’t know. Second, I will evaluate how much time and energy I have to do the relevant research. If it is in the not knowing what is unknown category, I will immediately ask for help. If I am down on time or energy, I will also ask for help. Otherwise, I will try to figure things out myself.

A description of what you expect from the facilitation team

I expect the team to help me in both tech and core by giving me the necessary amount of guidance. I hope the team can tell me both the positives and the negatives of what I am doing (or not doing).
